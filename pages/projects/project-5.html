Holographic Clock & Sea Level Forecaster

arduino, neopixel, climate awareness, physical computing

Year

2024

Tools

Arduino, NeoPixel Matrix, RTC Module, Ultrasonic Sensor

A dual-purpose interactive device that merges timekeeping with climate awareness. Using an 8x8 NeoPixel matrix and ultrasonic sensor, it switches between displaying current time and predicted years of sea level flooding for coastal cities, transforming everyday timekeeping into an urgent reminder of climate change impacts.
