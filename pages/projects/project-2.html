A Rajasthani-inspired virtual doorway blending the rich architecture and culture of India with modern technology. Users step through a culturally symbolic doorway where a pressure mat triggers visual and sound transformations from traditional Rajasthani motifs to abstract digital patterns, inviting reflection on cultural transitions and personal journeys.
